Flexible Monitoring Options

We strongly recommend cellular monitoring for all our security systems. Unlike landline monitoring, cellular communication uses 4G/LTE technology that can't be disabled by cutting phone lines, ensuring reliable alerts even if your landline is damaged or unavailable. Additionally, cellular-based or IP-based monitoring is required for access to Total Connect 2.0, our mobile app that provides complete system control, instant alerts, video feeds, and smart home integration from anywhere. These features are not available with traditional landline service. While we do offer landline monitoring options, cellular monitoring provides superior protection and is our preferred choice for comprehensive security coverage.

* All monitoring options are invoiced annually. If you decide to cancel your monitoring at anytime we require 30 days written notice prior to cancellation. We will then refund you the remainder owing for the rest of the year.*
